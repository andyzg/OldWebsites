Hi and welcome to the RScore calculator. This calculator will not only allow you to calculate your R-Score but also show you the programs you are potentially eligible for.


On the left hand side, you will be asked to input the CEGEP's high school average, your grade, the class average, the standard deviation and the course credit. You may add up to a total of 8 courses.


After adding all of the courses, click calculate and all of the programs you are eligible for will be listed to its associated University.


Please note that these are only the academic requirements. Some of these programs may require a letter of intent, a portfolio, interview, etc. The data used was from the admissions in 2012.


Click on HOME to start!
